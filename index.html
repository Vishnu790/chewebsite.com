Introduction

Forests provide many benefits to human society in addition to their central role as habitat and environmental regulators in natural ecosystems. These benefits are often described as natural resources that people can use for fuel, timber, and recreational or commercial purposes. The understanding that the forest provides people with natural resources was an important factor in the promotion of forest protection.
